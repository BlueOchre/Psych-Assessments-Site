Our commitment to cultural safety

We understand that a clinical assessment can feel daunting. We are deeply committed to providing a respectful, responsive, and culturally safe space for First Nations individuals and families across the Top End.

We routinely work alongside Aboriginal Interpreter Services (AIS) and national translation networks to ensure language is never a barrier to an accurate and fair assessment.
